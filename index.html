How to Use

Using TimeWorked is straightforward:

  • Enter times in 24-hour format (e.g., 09:00) or 12-hour format (e.g., 9:00 AM). The calculator accepts most common time formats.
  • Multiple shifts per day ‚Äî each day has up to three time slots for split shifts or breaks.
  • Set your hourly rate in the toolbar. Choose your currency and enter your rate.
  • Configure overtime by setting hours after which overtime kicks in and the multiplier (e.g., 1.5x after 8 hours).
  • Navigate weeks using the arrow buttons or click "Today" to jump to the current week.
  • Add notes for each day to track projects, tasks, or leave status.

Data Privacy

TimeWorked uses your browser's local storage to save your timesheet data. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device and is never sent to any server
  • No account creation or login required
  • Data persists between browser sessions on the same device
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your timesheet history
  • For permanent records, use the Export feature to download your data

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TimeWorked really free?

Yes, TimeWorked is completely free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or advertisements.

Can I access my timesheet from another device?

Since data is stored locally on your device, you'll need to export your data and import it on the other device, or use browser sync features if available.

What time formats are supported?

TimeWorked accepts 24-hour time (14:30), 12-hour time (2:30 PM), and shorthand formats (230p, 1430). Times are automatically normalized for calculations.

How is overtime calculated?

Overtime is calculated per day based on your threshold setting. Hours exceeding the threshold are multiplied by your overtime rate. Weekly overtime thresholds are not currently supported but may be added in a future update.
